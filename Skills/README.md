# Obsidian Ecosystem Radar — Claude Skills

Install these skills in Claude Desktop or Claude.ai to give your entire account team on-demand GTM intelligence. Each skill launches parallel subagents for speed — most complete in under 2 minutes.

**To install:** Claude.ai → Settings → Capabilities → Skills → Upload skill → select the `.zip` file.

---

## Account Research (`account-research.zip`)

**What it does:** Full pre-call intelligence briefing on any company. Launches 4 parallel subagents simultaneously — one researches the company overview and signals, one hunts economic buyers (CISO, CIO, VP Security), one hunts technical buyers (SOC, IAM, IR), and one maps the security vendor stack plus competitor and partner presence. Synthesizes everything into a structured briefing with Obsidian GTM angles, pillar alignment scores, and 5 specific recommended actions.

**When to use it:**
- Before any prospect or partner meeting
- When a new account lands in your territory and you need a fast read
- When a partner mentions an account and you want to know if competitors are already there
- Anytime you'd normally spend 45 minutes on LinkedIn and Google before a call

**How to invoke:** Just say — *"research Snowflake"* or *"scout Sutherland Global"* or *"prep me for my call with Workday tomorrow"*

---

## Account Brief (`account-brief.zip`)

**What it does:** Same 4-agent parallel research as Account Research, but instead of returning a chat summary it renders a complete **print-ready HTML file** — the full scout brief with company snapshot, signal cards, people with LinkedIn links, vendor stack table, pillar alignment, partner/competitive presence, keywords, and recommended actions. Save it as a `.html` file, open in browser, Cmd+P to PDF.

**When to use it:**
- When you need something to share, attach to an email, or bring into a QBR
- Before a partner briefing where you want to hand something over
- When you're presenting an account to your manager or SE and need a leave-behind
- Anytime Account Research isn't enough and you need a formatted artifact

**How to invoke:** Say — *"generate a brief for Citi"* or *"build me a one-pager on Sutherland Global"* or *"I need a brief before my call with ServiceNow"*

---

## URL Check (`url-check.zip`)

**What it does:** Drops two parallel subagents on a single URL — one fetches the page content, one searches for surrounding context. Returns a quick signal card: what's on the page, why it matters for Obsidian, which GTM pillar it maps to, a suggested action, and a ready-to-send outreach draft if the signal warrants one. Works on competitor blogs, partner press releases, news articles, analyst reports, or any account page.

**When to use it:**
- When a partner sends you a link and you want to know if there's an angle in it
- When you see a competitor press release and need a fast competitive read
- When someone forwards a news story about an account you're working
- When you're scanning your feed and something looks relevant but you don't want to read the whole thing

**How to invoke:** Paste the URL and say — *"check this URL"* or *"spot check this"* or just drop the link in the chat
