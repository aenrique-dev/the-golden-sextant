Partnerships & Alliances Obsidian Security March 2026 Revenue Impact

The Problem

1

Signal is everywhere, action is nowhere. Partner managers manually scan competitor blogs, marketplace listings, and alliance pages — but most signals never turn into pipeline.

2

Ecosystem moves faster than our coverage. Competitors announce new partner integrations, cloud co-sell plays, and MSSP motions that directly impact our partnerships — and we find out late or not at all.

3

The last mile is always missing. Even when a signal is spotted, there's no structured way to connect it to an account, a partner, and a recommended next action.

The Solution

Ecosystem Radar uses parallel AI research agents to continuously monitor competitors, partners, and market signals — then converts them into prioritized, actionable co-sell and pipeline opportunities.

Instead of reading 50 web pages, your partnership team gets a daily digest of the 5 signals that matter most — with "why it matters," which partner to activate, which accounts are affected, and what to do next. That turns passive intelligence into pipeline creation.
