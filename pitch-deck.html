How It Works
Scout. Interpret. Rank. Activate.
A four-stage AI pipeline runs on a schedule, reads the ecosystem so you don't have to, and delivers only what requires action.
Stage 1

Scout

Fetches 47+ pages in parallel — competitor blogs, partner sites, media, LinkedIn RSS, analyst sources. Strips noise. Pre-filters with Claude Haiku.

Stage 2

Interpret

Claude Sonnet reads each page through Obsidian's GTM lens. Extracts signals with title, type, summary, and why it matters for our pipeline.

Stage 3

Rank

Every signal scored HIGH / MEDIUM / LOW by urgency, competitive impact, and partner activation potential. Sorted before it reaches you.

Stage 4

Activate

For every HIGH/MEDIUM signal: which partner to call, what to say, and a ready-to-send outreach draft. Action, not information.
